Essential Info for Your Trip

  • 🌤️ Weather: Dubai has a desert climate with hot summers (up to 45°C) and mild winters (18–28°C).
  • 🏨 Accommodation: Dubai offers everything from budget-friendly stays to luxury 7-star hotels.
  • 🛬 Travel Accessibility: Well connected via metro, taxis, buses, airports, and even cruise ports.
  • 💰 Currency: The local currency is UAE Dirham (AED). 1 USD ≈ 3.67 AED.
  • 🛂 Visas: Most visitors can get a visa on arrival for 30–90 days. Check with your airline or embassy.
  • 🕰️ Best Time to Visit: November to March offers cooler weather, ideal for sightseeing and outdoor activities.
  • 🕌 Etiquette: Dubai is modern but rooted in Islamic culture. Dress modestly, especially in malls and public places.
  • 🧼 Safety: Dubai is extremely safe with strict laws and clean streets. Petty crime is rare.
  • 📶 Internet: Free Wi-Fi is widely available. Tourist SIM cards can be purchased at the airport or malls.
  • 👨‍👩‍👧‍👦 Family Friendly: Attractions like Dubai Aquarium, KidZania, and Legoland are perfect for children.
  • 📞 Emergency Numbers: Police: 999, Ambulance: 998, Tourist Helpline: 800 4888
